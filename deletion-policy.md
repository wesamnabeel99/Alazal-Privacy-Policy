# Data Deletion Policy

**Effective Date:** April 13, 2026

Alazal Platform respects your right to manage your personal data. This policy outlines how you can request the deletion of your account and associated personal information.

## 1. How to Request Data Deletion
You can request the deletion of your personal data using either of the following methods:

**Method 1: In-App Deletion**
1. Log in to your Alazal Platform account.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Account Management**.
3. Select **Delete Account**.
4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your request.

**Method 2: Email Request**
Send an email to alazalplatformdev@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include the email address and phone number associated with your account to help us verify your identity.

## 2. What Happens When You Request Deletion?
Upon receiving your verified request, we will initiate the deletion process:
* Your account will be permanently closed.
* Your personal data, including name, contact details, and learning progress, will be permanently deleted from our active databases within 14 days.
* You will lose access to all purchased courses, filmed lectures, and platform features.

## 3. Data Retention Exceptions
Please note that we may retain certain data for a longer period if required by law, or for legitimate business purposes such as resolving disputes, preventing fraud, and enforcing our terms of service. This retained data will be anonymized or isolated from further processing wherever possible.

## 4. Contact
For any questions regarding your data or this deletion policy, please contact us at: alazalplatformdev@gmail.com
